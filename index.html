How It Works

Setting up the testers
We’ll ask for tester-level access to your Google Play Console. This ensures everything’s set up and ready to go, so we can add our testers and start the process right away.
1
2
Closed testing starts
Within 24 hours, we’ll get all the testers on board and start testing your app. You’ll be the first to know when we’ve commenced the testing.
14 Days of Testing
Over 14 days, we’ll test your app daily, ensuring it meets Google’s guidelines for production readiness. During this time, you’ll need to release at least two updates—with or without any changes—essential for approval.
3
4
Ready for production
After 14 days, your app will be ready for production. We'll provide pre-written answers for Google's testing questions—just fill them out and submit. Production access is granted within 2-5 days after review.
